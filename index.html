OUR BATTLE AGAINST PLASTIC POLLUTION
Pollution generated by plastic waste is rapidly destroying our ecosystems over both land and sea. Join the fight to save our planet from harmful plastic pollution.

CHANGE STARTS WITH YOU

Every minute, one garbage truck of plastic is dumped into our oceans. If we continue at this rate, there will be more plastic in the ocean than fish by 2050. Change needs to happen now. Many people are unaware of the impact that they have on their environment, however the small efforts of one person to reduce plastic waste can turn into many. You can make a difference, take action now!

OUR MISSION STATEMENT

Our mission is to spread awareness regarding the negative impact of plastic pollution on the environment. By educating our website visitors on the effects of plastic across our land and oceans, we aim to inspire others to take action against the production of plastic waste and do their part to save our planet.
