FLASH FACT #1

Recent studies have revealed marine plastic pollution in 100% of marine turtles, 59% of whales, 36% of seals and 40% of seabird species examined.

FLASH FACT #2

There may now be around 5.25 trillion macro and microplastic pieces floating in the open ocean. Weighing up to 269,000 tons.

FLASH FACT #3

There is more microplastic in the ocean than there are stars in the Milky Way.

FLASH FACT #4

Plastics consistently make up 60 to 90% of all marine debris studied.

FLASH FACT #5

More than 8 million tons of plastic are dumped in our oceans every year.
